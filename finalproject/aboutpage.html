about my better halva

My Better Halva started as a neighborhood stand in my hometown in Virginia where people from across town would stop by for cheesy pide, kofte, and limonata made by my mother and I. I decided to follow my dreams and pursue food studies at Virginia Tech shortly after. Within a year of graduating that little neighborhood stand turned into a pop-up in Richmond where I served dozens of customers per day. I received numerous awards during this time, such as Pop-Up of the Year. in 2020. I made my transition into the restaurant scene right before the pandemic and continue to run My Better Halva with my partner. Now housed near Union Square, this family run restaurant strives to deliver high quality Turkish food to anyone with a hunger for good food and a good time.
